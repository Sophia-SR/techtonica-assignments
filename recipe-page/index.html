A Delicious Sweet Potato & Kale Curry Recipe!

I've always loved Indian food, and though this isn't the most authentic recipe - it's a tasty and simple interpretation that's super customizable. It takes less than 30 minutes to prepare, and perfect for the slow cooker too. Serve as is, or over some steamed basmati or quinoa, for a filling dinner that's sure to please the whole family!

Photo Credit: Kitchen Sanctuary

Ingredients:

  • 2 Large Sweet Potatoes chopped into large chunks.
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained.
  • 3 cups of roughly chopped Kale.
  • 2 tsps minced ginger.
  • 2 tsps minced garlic.
  • 1 red onion(chopped).
  • 2 Tbsp Sweet or Madras curry powder.
  • 1/8 tsp hing(asafoetida)(optional). click here for usage instructions.
  • 1 tsp ground coriander.
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper.
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp cumin.
  • 1 tsp chicken or vegetable boullion.
  • Sea Salt, to taste.
  • Olive Oil or Ghee.
  • 1/4 cup of Water.
  • Cilantro(optional).

Steps to Prep:

  1. In a medium sized saucepan or pot, saute your chopped Onion, Garlic, and Ginger together on medium heat until onion is translucent.
  2. Add all spices along with Chickpeas and saute on low heat until onions are caramelized and golden brown.
  3. Add Sweet Potatoes and Water, cover and steam on low heat until potates are tender.
  4. Add Kale, mix completely and cook until greens are tender.
  5. Season with additional Sea Salt if necessary, and serve!
